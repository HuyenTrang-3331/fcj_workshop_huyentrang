### Week 12 Achievements:

**Overall:**

In Week 12, I completed synthesizing and organizing all knowledge learned during the **First Cloud Journey** stage. This week's content helped me revisit the entire learning journey, from foundational AWS Cloud knowledge to important service groups like IAM, Networking, Compute, Storage, Database, Security, Monitoring, and Cost Optimization.

In addition to theoretical review, I also revisited completed labs, summarized acquired skills, prepared supporting images, finalized the report, and checked remaining AWS resources. Through this, I strengthened my self-study abilities, lab practice, architecture analysis, error checking, and technical report presentation skills.

  - Cost Optimization.

- Reviewed key networking components:

  - VPC.
  - Subnet.
  - Route Table.
  - Internet Gateway.
  - NAT Gateway.
  - Security Group.

- Reviewed database service roles:

  - Amazon RDS.
  - Amazon Aurora.
  - Amazon Redshift.
  - Amazon ElastiCache.
- Reinforced knowledge about backup, restore, and database migration during system operations.
- Reviewed monitoring and security service roles:

  - Amazon CloudWatch.
  - AWS CloudTrail.
  - Amazon GuardDuty.
  - AWS Security Hub.
  - AWS Config.
- Deepened understanding of **AWS Budgets**, **Billing**, and **Cost Explorer** roles in tracking, alerting, and cost optimization.

  - Enable MFA for important accounts.
  - Apply least privilege principle when granting permissions.
  - Don't expose unnecessary databases or backends publicly.

  - Monitor logs, metrics, and alarms during operations.
  - Clean up resources after labs to avoid unnecessary costs.
